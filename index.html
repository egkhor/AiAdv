Year 1 — AI as a Friend
Welcome to Year 1! Let's meet Aida! 👋
This is Aida — she lives inside phones and computers. Students discover AI everywhere in their daily life: from "Ok Google, play Negaraku" to Grab showing the fastest way home. Learn that AI needs electricity, information, and human-written rules to work.
  • A1
    AI Hunt at Home — Draw all things in your house that have AI inside
  • 🎮
    Simon Says with Aida — Play the classic game in the AR app (English & BM)
  • ✍️
    Year 1 Promise — Sign your first AI promise: "I will use AI to help people!"
🌟 Key Concept: What is AI?
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is a smart computer program that can learn and think a little like humans. It needs electricity, information, and human-written rules to work.
🏠 Where We Find AI
Google Assistant, YouTube recommendations, Smart TVs, Grab navigation — AI is all around us every day!
💛 Good Manners with AI
Even though AI cannot feel happy or sad, we still say "Please" and "Thank you". Good manners make the world better!

Year 2 — Patterns & Predictions
AI loves finding patterns! 🎯
Students learn how AI identifies patterns — Red, Blue, Red, Blue → next is Red! Through real Malaysian examples like flood prediction in Kelantan and beautiful batik design generation, children discover how AI learns from examples.
  • A2
    Pattern Train — Cut out cards, arrange animals/fruits, predict the next one

🌊 Malaysian Example: Flood Warning
In Kelantan, AI looks at river pictures every day. If the pattern says "water rising fast", it sends an SMS: "Be careful, flood may come!"
⚠️ Sometimes AI is Wrong!
If we give wrong examples, AI learns wrong things. That is why humans must teach carefully. We are responsible for what AI learns!

💬 What is a Prompt?
A prompt is a short instruction you give to AI. Example: "Write a story about a brave mouse in Penang." You are the boss — AI starts, YOU continue!
🦧 Try This Prompt!
"A little orangutan who loves nasi lemak" — what story would AI write? What would YOU add to the story?
⚠️ Remember
AI copies what it sees. We decide what it learns! Always choose kind, fair, and honest examples to guide your AI.

📜 Malaysia's 5 Big AI Rules
1. Fair to all races & religions • 2. Protect privacy • 3. No fake news • 4. Keep humans in control • 5. Help the environment
👹 The Bias Monster
If we only teach AI photos of rich people, it thinks everyone is rich. That is unfair! We must teach AI with diverse, representative examples.

AI can make fake videos of people saying things they never said. Always check before sharing! Be a responsible digital citizen.

AI Vocabulary

Learn the Language of AI

The book includes a comprehensive glossary of key AI terms — explained in simple, child-friendly language. Available in all 3 languages!

Algorithm 算法 / Algoritma
Steps a computer follows, like a recipe for AI.
Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Smart programs that can learn and think a little like humans.
Bias 偏见 / Bias
When AI is unfair because it learned wrong examples.
Chatbot 聊天机器人
A robot you can talk to on a computer or phone.
Computer Vision
AI that can "see" and understand pictures or videos.
Data 数据 / Data
All information (words, numbers, pictures) that AI uses to learn.
Deepfake 深度伪造
A fake video made by AI that looks 100% real.
Digital Footprint
Trail you leave online every time you click or type.
Ethics 伦理 / Etika
Knowing what is right and wrong when using AI.
Machine Learning
How AI gets smarter by looking at lots of examples.
Pattern 模式 / Corak
Things that repeat — AI loves finding patterns!
Privacy 隐私 / Privasi
Keeping your personal information safe and secret.
Prompt 提示 / Arahan
Short instruction you give to AI. "Draw a dragon eating durian!"
Training 训练 / Latihan
Teaching AI by showing it lots of examples.
Voice Assistant
AI you talk to — like Siri, Google Assistant, or "Ok Google".
